6 POPULAR WATCH TRENDS FOR 2018

Today, We want to get into the top 6 watch trends and definitely invest in, if you want to have a stylish wrist.


1.  CHRONOGRAPHS WATCHES


We think the most aesthetic part of it is the complications and just the busy dial itself. It makes the watch look more expensive. If you want the OG, or the must-need chronograph to have, it’s the Speedmaster Professional by Omega. But the only downside is, it’s like 4.5K, so it’s not realistic for the average guy. Which is why We love fashion watches. Vincero has their own chronograph model and We absolutely love it. They’re affordable watches that easily looks like a $600 watch and you’re only spending $100!


2.  ALL BLACK WATCHES


Not only major establishment brands are doing it like Panerai, Omega, Bell&Ross but fashion watches are hopping onto the trend as well. Vincero just dropped an all matte black chronograph model, it is so freakin’ sexy! Vincero watches are definitely unique, they bring that class and elegance from that high-end aesthetic with that affordable price.


3.  VINTAGE WATCHES


You can buy a vintage Rolex, that’s 30 or 40 years old and save yourself thousands of what it originally cost and you would still get that Rolex craftmanship. In addition, that watch could still last you another 10 to 20 years.


4.  APPLE WATCH


We’re not saying this should be your one and only watch, but it is a good watch to have in your collection. The functionality is great and it’s extremely popular right now. From the last statistics Apple released, they’re the #1 selling watch company, surpassing Rolex. So it’s hard to argue that it’s not a trend or it’s not popular.


5.  MINIMALIST WATCHES


Everyone from fashion to high end brands are doing it. They going towards something less cluttered and complicated. This is the one that can be your only watch. These watches go with every outfit. Of course, a great option is Nomos, but it’s gonna run you a couple thousand. On the other hand, Vincero has you covered, they also have a line of simple and minimal watches.


6.  FASHION WATCHES


Watch guys hate fashion watches. We definitely understand when they’re coming from. But We personally love them. Don’t get me wrong, We have the high-end stuff and We know the quality behind it. But at those prices, they just can’t compete with fashion watches. Like We were saying, We’re not telling you that you should or should not invest in fashion watches but it is something that is most certainly trending.
